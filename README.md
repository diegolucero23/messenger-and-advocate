### Historical Context
These documents shed light on a pivotal era in Latter Day Saint history, specifically the succession crisis. They detail Sidney Rigdon's perspective on:
* The succession claims regarding Brigham Young and the Twelve.
* The introduction of "Spiritual Wifery" and plural marriage.
* The "Cochranism" controversy.
* The murders of Joseph and Hyrum Smith.
* Allegations of adultery and "secret priesthood" activities in Nauvoo.

By modernizing these transcripts, this project aims to make this primary source material accessible for historical analysis, search indexing, and academic research.
